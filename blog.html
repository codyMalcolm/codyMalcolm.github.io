How I got to here

My current curriculum:

  • freeCodeCamp.org - This is where I started, and I am just wrapping up the Front End Libraries Certification. I have made a personal commitment to complete at least one new lesson or project a day, even if I am focusing on different courses at the time. Track my profile.
  • Wes Bos' 30 day Javascript coding challenge - As of July 28th, I have completed up to day 6 of Wes Bos's program and every day I learn a lot of new techniques. This course is exceeding my wildest expectations. I will definitely take several of Wes' other courses once this one is completed.
  • Codecademy.com - I have taken several courses on codecademy, including "Introduction to JavaScript" and "Learn the command line." I find the frequent hands-on "PRO" projects very helpful to reinforce practical application of the taught concepts. Generally, once I have completed my other daily commitments and any other targeted learning, I spend the rest of my time working on a codecademy course. I plan to complete the React courses and then the JQuery course next, and then continue working on the freeCodecamp curriculum as a priority.
  • Dave Geddes' Flexbox Zombies - This "course" makes mastering flexbox fun. At the author's recommendation, I do only one chapter a day to take advantage of the benefits of spaced repetition.
